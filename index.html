Our Story

A little café with a lot of heart

Tucked just off York Road, Side Street Cafe has been a quiet anchor of the Cockeysville community for years. Owner-run and deeply personal, this is the kind of place where the staff already knows your order by your third visit.

Step inside and you're greeted by warm green walls, shelves lined with antiques and mismatched plates, and the smell of something simmering on the stove. It's a space that feels lived-in — and that's entirely by design. Whether you're slipping away for a solo lunch, catching up over a bowl of chili, or treating a client to something genuinely memorable, Side Street delivers without pretension.

The menu is straightforward and executed with care: scratch-made soups, generously built sandwiches, and unexpected specials that reflect the seasons. No shortcuts, no freezer shortcuts — just honest food made by people who take pride in it.
